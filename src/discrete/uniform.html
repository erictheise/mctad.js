Uniform Distribution (Discrete)

mctad.discreteUniform() accepts two Integers, i and j, the lower and upper bounds of a range of equally likely integers, and returns an Object containing the mean, median, mode, variance, skewness, entropy,domain, P (the probability mass function, P(X)), F (the cumulative distribution function, F(X)), and generate(), a function for generating n random variables using the specified distribution.
