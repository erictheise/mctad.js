A factorial, usually written n!, is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to n.

Factorial

The Factorial is the product of all positive integers up to a specified odd positive integer. The mathematical notation is n!.

Assumptions

n is a non-negative Integer.

Use

mctad.factorial(n)
